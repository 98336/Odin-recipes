Palak Paneer

Palak Paneer is a popular North Indian vegetarian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a smooth, vibrant green spinach gravy. This healthy and flavorful dish is seasoned with aromatic spices and is often enjoyed with naan, roti, or rice.

Ingredients:

  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  • Fresh spinach leaves
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Green chilies
  • Cumin seeds
  • Turmeric powder
  • Garam masala
  • Red chili powder
  • Fresh cream (optional)
  • Salt
  • Oil or ghee

Steps:

  1. Blanch the spinach leaves in boiling water, then blend into a smooth paste.
  2. Fry cumin seeds in oil, add chopped onions, garlic, ginger, and green chilies, and saut√© until golden.
  3. Add tomatoes and spices, cook until tomatoes are soft.
  4. Add the spinach puree and paneer cubes, cook for a few minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh cream and serve hot with naan or rice.
